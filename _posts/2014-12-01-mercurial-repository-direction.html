I had forgotten that the command order dictates the direction of the clone ie from my local to remote repo or remote to local repo
local -> remote
hg clone . ssh://user@usersURL.org/hg/myrepos/NewRepo
 remote -> local
 hg clone ssh://user@usersURL.org/hg/myrepos/NewRepo .
